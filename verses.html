• 'Love
  • To love is to build a house of cards, or play a game of chess
  • But one wrong word or ill-thought move
  • And you must start it all afresh.
  • Tide
  • Whenever I watch the tide recede
  • Cold coils of fear grip round my heart.
  • Will the seas sneak back, calm and sure
  • In the dark of night as they have before?
  • Or will they stay on distant shores
  • Leaving crushed shells and washed-up dreams
  • As memory of surfs of yore?'

The Collected Verse of Gonzal de Verceo

One guaranteed copy of this book is found in a chest at the Royal Palace in Vizima, in the room where Geralt and Yennefer meet during the quest Imperial Audience.
